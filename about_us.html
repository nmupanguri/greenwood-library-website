Welcome to Greenwood Library

Greenwood Library is a community-focused institution dedicated to providing access to information, resources, and educational opportunities to our patrons.

Our library offers a wide range of services including:

  • Book borrowing and returns
  • Access to computers and internet
  • Reference assistance
  • Community events and programs

We strive to create a welcoming environment for individuals of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply an avid reader, Greenwood Library has something for you.
